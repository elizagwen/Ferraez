Practice Areas

At Ferraez and Associates, we specialize in a range of legal services to provide the representation you deserve. Explore our key practice areas below:

  • Personal Injury

  • DUI / DWI

  • Bad Faith Insurance

  • Property Damage

  • Business Litigation

  • Estate Litigation

Personal Injury

At Ferraez and Associates, we advocate for individuals who have suffered harm due to another party's negligence. Whether you’ve been injured in a car accident, slip-and-fall, or another preventable incident, we are dedicated to securing compensation for your medical bills, lost income, and emotional suffering. Our mission is to help you recover and rebuild your life after an unexpected setback.

DUI / DWI

Facing DUI or DWI charges can be a stressful and life-altering experience. Our legal team is committed to protecting your rights and building a strong defense. Whether it’s challenging the legality of traffic stops, contesting evidence, or negotiating reduced penalties, we work tirelessly to minimize the impact on your future and reputation.

Bad Faith Insurance

When insurance companies fail to uphold their commitments by denying valid claims or delaying payouts, your financial stability is at stake. At Ferraez and Associates, we hold insurers accountable for their bad faith practices, ensuring you receive the compensation you’re entitled to. We stand by your side to restore fairness and justice in your time of need.

Property Damage

Property damage can disrupt your life and create unexpected financial strain. Whether it’s caused by natural disasters, accidents, or disputes with insurers, we are here to help. Our firm specializes in navigating the complexities of property claims, ensuring you are fairly compensated and able to restore or replace what matters most to you.

Business Litigation

In the competitive world of business, disputes can arise over contracts, partnerships, or other commercial relationships. Our attorneys provide effective legal solutions to resolve conflicts, protect your interests, and preserve your business's integrity. From negotiation to litigation, we deliver strategic representation tailored to your goals.

Estate Litigation

Estate disputes often involve high stakes and deep emotions, especially when conflicts arise over wills, trusts, or the administration of assets. Our firm handles these complex cases with care and precision, advocating for fair resolutions while preserving relationships. We work to ensure that your loved one’s wishes are honored and your family’s legacy is protected.

About Cory Ferraez

Growing up in the charm of Columbus, Mississippi, Cory’s upbringing was steeped in tradition and refinement, influenced notably by his grandfather, who owned a renowned men’s clothing store. This upbringing instilled in him a sense of etiquette and sophistication that echoes in his personal, legal, and professional lives.

To help those that can’t help themselves is the bedrock with which Cory began his law career and the foundation on which he defends his clients. Tough and demanding are words that can be used to describe law in Mississippi – and that’s why he does it. Cory values friends above all else. In some instances, the bond of a friendship can be stronger than that of a family. Those bonds and a passion for helping others thrive is what his practice is made of, right here in Mississippi.
